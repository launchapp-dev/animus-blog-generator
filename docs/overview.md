# Animus Blog Generator

## What it does

Animus Blog Generator is an open-source, fully automated blog pipeline. You configure it once for your business — niche, audience, voice, competitors — and it handles the entire content lifecycle:

**Discovers** what to write about (Search Console gaps, competitor analysis, forum mining, industry news)
**Researches** with real data (scrapes reports, market data, government records — not hallucinated stats)
**Writes** 2,000+ word posts in your brand voice with proper citations
**Optimizes** for both traditional SEO and AI search citations (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews)
**De-AI-ifies** the content — a dedicated agent strips AI writing patterns and injects human voice
**Generates** featured images and platform-specific social content (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn)
**Monitors** published posts and automatically refreshes declining ones with updated data

Runs on a schedule (2x/week publishing, weekly refresh, daily news scan) with zero manual intervention.

## Why it's better

**Most AI blog tools are single-shot** — paste a topic, get a generic post. This is a multi-agent pipeline where 7 specialized agents each handle what they're best at. The strategist picks topics differently than the writer writes, and the SEO optimizer catches things the writer misses.

**Research-first, not vibes-first.** Agents scrape real sources (Firecrawl, Exa, Brave, Google Maps, Search Console) before writing a single word. Every claim gets a citation. No "studies show" without the actual study. Optionally plug in your own content "knowledge brain" (a bring-your-own content MCP) to ground every draft in your org's accumulated context — prior research, established facts, angles already used — so posts stay unique and never repeat themselves.

**AI SEO is built in.** Most tools optimize for Google circa 2020. This pipeline structures content to get *cited* by AI systems — extractable chunks, attributed statistics, Q&A sections, schema markup. That's the game now.

**The refresh cycle is unique.** It doesn't just publish and forget. It monitors Search Console, identifies declining posts, analyzes what competitors improved, and automatically rewrites with fresh data. Your content stays current without you touching it.

**It's YAML, not a black box.** Every agent prompt, every phase, every schedule is in a single readable YAML file. Swap models, add phases, change the voice — it's your pipeline. No vendor lock-in, no monthly SaaS fee.
